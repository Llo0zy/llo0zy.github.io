Bandit 17 / Overthewire

octubre 29, 2023

  • bandit
  • ssh

  • linux

Información

*There are 2 files in the homedirectory: passwords.old and passwords.new. The password for the next level is in passwords.new and is the only line that has been changed between passwords.old and passwords.new

NOTE: if you have solved this level and see ‘Byebye!’ when trying to log into bandit18, this is related to the next level, bandit19*

Resolución

Nos esta diciendo que la contraseña esta almacenada en passwords.new y es la única diferente de password.old, para esto usaremos el comando: diff:

bandit17@bandit:~$ diff passwords.new passwords.old 
42c42
< hga5tuuCLF6fFzUpnagiMN8ssu9LFrdg
---
> glZreTEH1V3cGKL6g4conYqZqaEj0mte

Siendo la contraseña de bandit18 : hga5tuuCLF6fFzUpnagiMN8ssu9LFrdg.

Nos aparecerá: ByeBye! al intentar loggearnos, eso es parte del próximo nivel.
